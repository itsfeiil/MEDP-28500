What to do

Red Velvet Brownie

  • Preheat the oven to 350 F (177 C). Line an 8x8 pan with parchment paper along the bottom and two sides.
  • Add the unsalted butter to a heat safe bowl. Heat the butter until fully melted.
  • Whisk the red gel food coloring, vanilla extract and vinegar until fully combined. Whisk in the eggs and cocoa powder until well combined. Whisk in the flour until just combined.
  • Evenly pour the brownie batter into the prepared pan.
  • Bake for about 25 -30 minutes. Cool the brownies in the pan for 30 minutes. Remove the brownies from the pan to cool completely before frosting with the cream cheese icing.

Cream Cheese Frosting

  • Beating the butter and cream cheese together until fluffy and well combined.
  • Pour in the vanilla extract, powdered sugar and 1 teaspoon milk. Beat the cream cheese frosting until smooth and well combined.

What is it

Red Velvet Brownies are the perfect combination of fudgy brownies and red velvet cake. Red Velvet Cake was originated in the South of the USA during the Great Depression. The best part of Red Velvet Cake is the tanginess you get from the buttermilk and vinegar. It is "The Brownie” of New York for over 50 years, made with Greenberg’s custom blend of fudgy red velvet with a thick ribbon of luscious cheesecake. With a striking red crumb and a thick layer of luxurious cream cheese frosting on top, they are a delicious anytime treat. These red velvet brownies will satisfy two sweet tooth cravings at once!
