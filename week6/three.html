What is a Rectangle

A rectangle is a closed 2-D shape, having 4 sides, 4 corners, and 4 right angles (90°). The opposite sides of a rectangle are equal and parallel. Since, a rectangle is a 2-D shape, it is characterized by two dimensions, length, and width. Length is the longer side of the rectangle and width is the shorter side. Rectangle being the most common shape forms a part of our day to day life. Some real-life examples of the rectangle are given below.

Must have properties

It has 4 sides and 4 vertices.
All the angles of a rectangle are 90°.
Opposite sides of a rectangle are equal and Parallel.
The sum of all the interior angles is 360°.
Diagonals of a rectangle bisect each other.
