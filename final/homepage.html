FORTUNE TELLING in Different Culture

WHAT IS DIVINATION

Divination, the practice of determining the hidden significance or cause of events, sometimes foretelling the future, by various natural, psychological, and other techniques. It can be diagnostic, forecasting, and interventionist, in the sense of changing the receptor’s destiny.

Divination have many forms, including augury, pyromancy, hydromancy, cleromancy, geomancy, somatomancy, oneiromancy, astrology, and tarot cards.

CHINESE FORTUNE-TELLINGS

Palmistry/shǒuxiàng

Using the palm of one's hand to predict one's destiny, analyzes the positioning of palm lines for love, personality, and other traits of your future. Chinese palmistry closely resembles Western palmistry in technique, using the seven major lines found on palms to gather the most important information about one's future.

WESTERN FORTUNE-TELLINGS

Tarot Cards

The most well-known method of divination.
Tarot card decks have been used for divination since the early 15th century.
Originally serving as playing cards, the decks begin to be used for cartomancy, or fortune telling, around the mid-18th century.
