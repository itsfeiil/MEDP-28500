ABOUT

Sage green is an earth-toned The color’s name is inspired by the leaves of the sage plant, a popular culinary spice. , greyish green with the hex code #8A9A5B, made by mixing equal parts citron and slate in the RGB color model. The word sage is defined as a profoundly wise person—someone who is venerated for his wisdom, judgment, and experience. Sage green is, thus, a color that can express wisdom. As a green, it also communicates peace and growth and speaks of ecology and nature.

SAGE GREEN IN CODING

In a RGB color space (made from three colored lights for red, green, and blue), hex #b2ac88 is made of 69.8% red, 67.5% green and 53.3% blue. In a CMYK color space (also known as process color, or four color, and used in color printing), hex #b2ac88 is made of 0% cyan, 3% magenta, 24% yellow and 30% black. Sage Green has a hue angle of 51.4 degrees, a saturation of 21.4% and a lightness of 61.6%.
